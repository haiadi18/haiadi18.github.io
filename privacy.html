Privacy Policy

MultiplyGo – Multiplication Table App

Last updated: May 28, 2026

MultiplyGo ("the App") is a multiplication table learning app designed for children and families. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

The App does not require registration, login, or any personal information to use.

All data generated by the App is stored locally on your device only, including:

  • Quiz history and scores
  • Progress and mastery data per multiplication table
  • App settings (theme, language, notifications preference)
  • Profile name and avatar selection

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Avatar Images

The App offers illustrated robot avatars generated by DiceBear (dicebear.com). When you view or select an avatar, a request is made to the DiceBear API (api.dicebear.com) to fetch the image. No personal information is included in this request — only a non-identifiable seed word (e.g. "Luna", "Felix"). Please refer to DiceBear's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

If you choose to use a photo from your camera or gallery as an avatar, that image is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local push notifications as study reminders. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve any external server. You can disable notifications at any time in the App settings or your device settings.

Children's Privacy

MultiplyGo is designed for use by children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or anyone else. No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or tracking services.

The only external network request is the optional DiceBear avatar image fetch described above.

Data Security

All app data is stored using your device's standard local storage (AsyncStorage). We do not transmit or store any data on external servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
